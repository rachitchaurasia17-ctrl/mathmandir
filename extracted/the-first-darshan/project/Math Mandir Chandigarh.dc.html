01 — Why Visit

A Divine Sanctuary in the
Heart of the City

In the peaceful environs of Sector 20, the Sri Chaitanya Gaudiya Math stands as a beacon of spiritual solace, devotion, and community. Meticulously kept gardens, the gentle fragrance of incense, and the distant hum of kirtan create an atmosphere made for reflection and prayer.

Whether you seek a moment of quiet meditation or wish to join the vibrant daily aratis, the temple offers a welcoming space for all sincere seekers of truth.
