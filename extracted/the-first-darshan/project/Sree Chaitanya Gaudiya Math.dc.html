Chapter I · The Appearance

When light descended
upon Nabadwip

In the golden dusk of 1486, the Supreme Lord descended as Sri Krishna Chaitanya Mahaprabhu — born Vishvambhar Mishra in Navadvipa, West Bengal. He came not to be worshipped, but to teach the world how to love: the congregational chanting of the Holy Name, the Yuga-dharma for this age.

श्री चैतन्य महाप्रभु ने इस कलियुग में हरिनाम संकीर्तन के माध्यम से प्रेमभक्ति का प्रचार किया — जीवमात्र के प्रति करुणा ही उनकी शिक्षा है।
